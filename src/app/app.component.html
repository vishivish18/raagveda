About Us

HI There, Welcome to our page, Raagveda ! (formlery The Tender Mango Project). We are a bunch of working professionals and musicians bonded by our love of music. We are the misfits obssessed with the love of music above all. We travel and party together while making some beautiful music and life time memories. We love to experiment and will keep sharing and creating music that touches souls. If you like our efforts please like our videos and subscribe to our channel. It will encourage us to keep creating this music.
